Portfolio Topic 2

Creating ML Model on Calculating Delay of Trains on Various Factors:

Designed and implemented a machine learning model to predict train delays based on various factors such as weather conditions, infrastructure issues, and historical data. The model leverages advanced regression techniques and data analysis to accurately forecast potential delays, enabling railway authorities to proactively manage schedules, optimize resources, and minimize disruptions. By harnessing the power of machine learning, the model empowers decision-makers to make informed operational decisions and improve the reliability of train services.

Portfolio Topic 3

Creating Air Pollution Prediction Using LSTM:

Developed an innovative LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) based model for predicting air pollution levels in urban areas. By analyzing historical air quality data and environmental factors such as weather patterns and traffic congestion, the model generates accurate forecasts of pollution levels for different time intervals. This predictive capability enables city planners, policymakers, and environmental agencies to implement targeted interventions, mitigate pollution-related health risks, and enhance overall air quality management strategies.
